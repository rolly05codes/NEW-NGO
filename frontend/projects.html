Successful Disaster Response Operations

Flood Relief - 2023 Telangana Operation

Challenge: Massive flooding affected 25,000+ people in rural Telangana, leaving communities stranded and without access to basic necessities.

Our Response: Deployed 200+ volunteers across 15 relief camps providing emergency food, water, shelter, and medical aid.

Results:

  • ✓ 500+ families provided shelter
  • ✓ 10,000+ meals distributed
  • ✓ 150+ medical cases treated
  • ✓ ₹50 Lakh relief provided

Earthquake Recovery - 2022 Hyderabad

Challenge: 6.2 magnitude earthquake caused damage to 500+ homes and critical infrastructure.

Our Response: Launched immediate rescue operations followed by long-term reconstruction program.

Results:

  • ✓ 200+ people rescued
  • ✓ 150 homes rebuilt
  • ✓ 50 schools reconstructed
  • ✓ ₹1 Crore invested in recovery

Cyclone Response - 2023 Coastal Areas

Challenge: Severe cyclone threatened coastal communities with 100+ km/h winds and storm surge.

Our Response: Coordinated evacuation, provided early warnings, and post-disaster rehabilitation.

Results:

  • ✓ 8,000+ people successfully evacuated
  • ✓ 0 casualties due to early warning
  • ✓ 100+ days rescue operations
  • ✓ ₹75 Lakh emergency aid
