About YRDS

Committed to Disaster Management and Community Development Since 2010

Our Story

Yashwant Rural Development Society was founded with a mission to provide comprehensive disaster management and relief services to rural communities. Over the past years, we have evolved into one of the most trusted NGOs in disaster response and recovery.

Our organization believes that every life is precious, and every community deserves immediate support during their time of crisis. We work tirelessly to ensure that affected populations receive emergency relief while building resilience for the future.

Through our integrated approach combining emergency response, recovery, and preparedness, we have successfully served thousands of families across multiple states.

Vision & Mission

🎯

Our Vision

A world where communities are prepared for disasters, resilient during crises, and empowered in recovery. We envision a future where no one faces disaster alone.

🎖️

Our Mission

To provide timely, effective, and compassionate disaster relief while building long-term community resilience through preparedness, response, and recovery programs.
