Disaster Recovery & Management Framework

Our comprehensive approach to disaster management covers all phases from prevention to recovery

🛡️

Prevention & Preparedness

Early warning systems, community training, disaster drills, and awareness campaigns to build community resilience

  • ✓ Risk assessment and mapping
  • ✓ Community awareness programs
  • ✓ Disaster preparedness training
  • ✓ Infrastructure strengthening
🚨

Response & Relief

Immediate action during disaster to save lives, provide emergency shelter, food, water, and medical assistance

  • ✓ Search and rescue operations
  • ✓ Emergency medical camps
  • ✓ Food and water distribution
  • ✓ Temporary shelter provision
🏗️

Recovery & Rehabilitation

Medium-term reconstruction of infrastructure, psycho-social support, and restoring normal life conditions

  • ✓ Infrastructure rebuilding
  • ✓ Counseling and trauma support
  • ✓ Housing restoration
  • ✓ Community rehabilitation programs
📈

Development & Resilience

Long-term development with disaster-resistant structures, livelihood support, and building a resilient society

  • ✓ Livelihood restoration programs
  • ✓ Disaster-resistant reconstruction
  • ✓ Education and skill development
  • ✓ Long-term community support
