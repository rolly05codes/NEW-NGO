Disaster Preparedness & Awareness

Be prepared. Be safe. Be resilient.

Preparedness Guides

⛰️

Earthquake Preparedness

Do's: Take cover under sturdy furniture, move away from windows, practice drop-cover-hold drill.

Don'ts: Don't run outside, don't use elevators, don't stand in doorways.

🌊

Flood Preparedness

Do's: Move to higher ground, listen to weather alerts, turn off utilities.

Don'ts: Don't drive through flooded areas, don't wade in flood water, don't touch power lines.

🌀

Cyclone Preparedness

Do's: Evacuate early, secure loose objects, stay in a safe room.

Don'ts: Don't go outside during the storm, don't ignore evacuation orders, don't rely on outdated information.

🔥

Fire Safety

Do's: Evacuate immediately, feel doors for heat, crawl under smoke.

Don'ts: Don't hide, don't use elevators, don't go back for belongings.

Evacuation Planning

Create Your Evacuation Plan

1. Identify Evacuation Routes

Know at least two ways to leave your home and neighborhood.

2. Gather and Organize Documents

Keep important documents in a waterproof container.

3. Plan for Vulnerable Persons

Make arrangements for elderly, children, and those with disabilities.

4. Practice Your Plan

Conduct drills with your family members regularly.
