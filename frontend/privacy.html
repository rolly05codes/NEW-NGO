Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Yashwant Rural Development Society ("we", "us", "our", "NGO", or "Organization") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you donate, volunteer, or request help, we collect your name, email, phone number, address, and related information.

Payment Information: Payment details are processed securely through third-party payment gateways. We do not store credit card information.

Website Usage: We collect information about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • ✓ To process donations and generate receipts
  • ✓ To manage volunteer registrations and send updates
  • ✓ To respond to help requests and provide assistance
  • ✓ To send important updates and newsletters
  • ✓ To improve our website and services
  • ✓ To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Our website uses SSL encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • ✓ Government agencies for disaster relief operations
  • ✓ Payment processors for transaction processing
  • ✓ Partner NGOs for coordinated relief efforts
  • ✓ Legal authorities when required by law

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✓ Access your personal information
  • ✓ Request correction of inaccurate data
  • ✓ Request deletion of your data
  • ✓ Opt-out of communications
  • ✓ Request data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
